STARawards 2020 are up!

What are the ESN STARawards?

The STARawards is a project aimed at recognition and appreciation of our volunteers’ work. Each year there are several categories in which Local and National organisations can submit their initiative/ activity/ event/ project/ programme/ campaign. The best applications are awarded a certificate signed by the president of ESN AISBL. This certificate can be used towards the universities or other stakeholders.

How are the applications evaluated?

The Local or National organisations have to submit an application describing their initiative/ activity/ event/ project/ programme/ campaign focusing on the core idea, execution, outcomes and the impact on the target group. The applications are then evaluated by a jury consisting of the members of the Network Committee and members of other international teams active in the respective category’s field.
